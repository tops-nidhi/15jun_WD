Birds List

American Dipper
The American dipper is almost always seen in or along rivers and streams, where it frequently bobs or "dips" its body up and down as it searches for food.
Arctic Tern
The arctic tern is a slender bird with narrow wings and short legs.
Northern Pintail
The pintail, also called the northern pintail, is a fast and graceful flyer that can be recognized by its long, thin neck and slim body.
